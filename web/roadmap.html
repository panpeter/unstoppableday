Roadmap

Below is a loose roadmap. It's split into three initial phases.

Phase 1 (current)

The goal of phase 1 is to launch day NFTs.

Each day will be represented by an ERC-721 token. The day owner will have certain responsibility - to promote the day across their network. As a reward, they will keep receiving UDAY coins proportionally to the community engagement.

Naturally, upcoming days will be more popular, so they will have a higher value. Famous past days (and also future ones) may be valuable too.

The minting fee will be only $10. Ideally, it should be free, but we need a relatively small amount to prevent abuse and promote fair distribution.

Phase 2

Phase 2 goal is to launch events and UDAY coin.

An event will also be an NFT and everyone can create one for free. Every event will be associated with a single day and others can "like" or upvote events. Authors of the most popular events will be rewarded with UDAY coin.

There will be also a few spots on the day page for "promoted" events. The event promoter will have to acquire and spend UDAY coins which will then get burned. Events burning the most UDAYs will win the place in promo spots in an auction-like manner.

This will create a demand for UDAY and its price will be naturally settled by the market. Active users will be able to sell rewarded UDAYs and promoters will be able to use them to promote their events and support unstoppableday.io community.

Phase 3

Phase 3 goal is to launch threads and comments.

When completed, everyone will be able to comment and start new threads. Similarly, as with events, the most interesting content will be rewarded with UDAY coins by the community using the voting system.

This will significantly increase the engagement of the unstoppableday.io community and trigger a conversion from a calendar to a fully-fledged social platform.

Future

After Phase 3 unstoppableday.io platform should host a thriving crypto community. It will be a daily go-to place for everyone to check what's new in crypto and discuss the latest events.

But in the future, unstoppableday.io will grow and spread into other topics. After seeing the power of this decentralized platform, new communities will naturally form around other interests. It's also very likely that there will be a DAO dedicated to growing and managing the platform.
